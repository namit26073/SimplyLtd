Launched in 2021 on the strength of demand from the Falafel queue. Same truck team, same standards,
different cuisine.

Shawarma is a fast-food discipline. Marinades go in early, the meat turns all day, the wrap goes
together in under ninety seconds, and we serve until the queue clears.
