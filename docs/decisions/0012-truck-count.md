# ADR 0012 — Truck count

**Date:** 2026-05-17
**Status:** Accepted

## Decision

The site commits to **three** trucks at Paddington Basin:

- **Simply Falafel** + **Simply Shawarma** share Merchant Square, W2 1PW (approx 51.5193, -0.1768).
- **Simply Burgers** at Canal Side Walk, Paddington Basin, W2 1AS (approx 51.5187, -0.1748).

The "seven food trucks" claim on the existing Wix site is **not** carried forward.

## Why

- Confirmed operational reality on the ground today, per the owner (Namit, via brief, 2026-05-16).
- The Wix site's "seven trucks" claim is unsubstantiated — no pitch addresses, no rota, no map. Likely aspirational or a count of concept lines rather than physical units.
- Truthful = sustainable. Easier to add later than to retract.

## Open thread

If the owner subsequently confirms additional pitches (e.g. Pasta and Lebanese), they become new entries in the `locations` content collection. Schema supports N pitches; today there are 2.
